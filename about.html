About WordleLike

The ultimate free destination for daily word puzzles and brain games.

Our Story

WordleLike was born from a simple observation: word puzzle fans deserve better. We loved playing Wordle, mini crosswords, and word games, but we kept hitting paywalls, ad clutter, and limited daily plays. So we built our own.

Every puzzle on WordleLike is generated fresh by AI, which means there's always something new to play. No recycled content, no stale puzzles — just a never-ending stream of word fun.

What We Offer

  • WordleLike — The classic 5-letter word guessing game
  • Word Hurdle — A tougher 6-letter variant
  • Mini Crossword — Quick daily crossword puzzles
  • Word Search — Find hidden words in the grid
  • Anagram Solver — Unscramble letters to form words

Why We're Different

  • 🤖 AI-Powered — Every puzzle is unique, generated fresh daily
  • 💰 100% Free — No paywalls, no subscriptions, no hidden fees
  • 🔒 No Sign-Up — Just click and play, we never ask for personal info
  • 📱 Works Everywhere — Desktop, tablet, phone — any browser
  • 🏆 Leaderboards — Compete with friends and see your ranking

Tech Stack

WordleLike is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks, no bloat. Deployed on Vercel with Cloudflare CDN. Daily puzzles are generated via AI API, fully automated through GitHub Actions.
