What Is Word Hurdle?

Word Hurdle is a challenging 6-letter word game for players who find Wordle too easy. With an extra letter to guess, every round becomes a tougher test of your vocabulary.

Strategy Tips

  • Start with words that have common vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
  • Use consonants like T, N, S, R in your first guess
  • Pay attention to letter patterns - 6-letter words often end in -ING, -TION, -ED
