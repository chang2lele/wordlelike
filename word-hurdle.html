What Is Word Hurdle?

Word Hurdle is a challenging 6-letter word game for players who find Wordle too easy. With an extra letter to guess, every round becomes a tougher test of your vocabulary and word-solving skills.

Unlike standard 5-letter word games, Word Hurdle requires you to think about longer words with more complex letter patterns. The 6x6 grid gives you six attempts to crack the daily puzzle — but those six letters open up exponentially more possibilities than five.

How Word Hurdle Differs From Wordle

The core gameplay is similar: guess the hidden word, get color-coded feedback. But that extra letter changes everything. Common 5-letter words like "APPLE" or "TRAIN" aren't options anymore — you're hunting for words like "BETTER", "PUZZLE" or "GARDEN". The difficulty jump is significant but rewarding.

Strategy Tips for 6-Letter Word Games

  • Start vowel-heavy: Words like "AUDIO", "ADIEU", or "OUNCE" reveal multiple vowels in one guess
  • Watch common prefixes: RE-, UN-, IN-, DIS-, and PRE- appear frequently in 6-letter words
  • Look for suffixes: -ING, -TION, -ED, -ER, -LY are your friends
  • Use high-value consonants: T, N, S, R, L, C in your first 2-3 guesses eliminate the most possibilities
  • Don't repeat gray letters: Every wasted guess costs you in a 6-try game
